The course

AI Career Readiness: five modules, one journey

A practical, self-paced, mobile-friendly course on the ASEAN Digital Academy, organised by the ASEAN Foundation and supported by LinkedIn. It shows you how to use AI as a powerful tool to build your career. It is designed for all youth across ASEAN with a particular focus on university and TVET students and NEET.

5modules × 3 parts each
60+minutes of core content
1 yrLinkedIn Premium on completion
  1. AI for Career Clarity & Professional Development Pathway
  2. AI-Powered Personal Branding & LinkedIn Optimization
  3. AI for Job Search, Networking & Opportunity
  4. AI for CV, Portfolio & Strong Application
  5. AI for Interview Preparation & Confidence Building
Completion means all three: finish 100% of the modules, create or join at least one discussion, and complete the Post-Course Evaluation. Only then do the certificate and LinkedIn Premium unlock.
Completion also opens what comes after the course: the Peer Mentorship component and Virtual Job Fair events, offered to a select number of completers. See what's next →

Core principles

Every event follows the same four rules

  1. Standard agenda, always

    Every event—in-person, webinar, hybrid, or asynchronous—follows the same agenda structure, so every participant gets the same experience and the program gets the same data.

  2. The course is the core

    Events exist to get participants through the AI Career Readiness Course to full completion—not just attendance.

  3. Budget realistic time

    The core content includes videos, assessment tests, and activities. Plan a synchronous event accordingly.

  4. Every participant is accounted for

    A complete participant list is mandatory for every event—no list, no event. It drives completion monitoring, verification, and reporting.
