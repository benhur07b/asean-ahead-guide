Self-paced

Can't make any event? Go self-paced.

The self-paced track is open for the entire programme. Sign up whenever you're ready and complete the course at your own pace (preferred within 1 month), with a facilitator backing you until you finish.

  1. Register for the self-paced track

    Use the registration link above — it's also listed in the schedule under Self-paced.

  2. Enroll in ASEAN Digital Academy

    Create your account on ada.aseanfoundation.org using an active email, then enroll in the AI Career Readiness course.

  3. Work through the course at your own pace

    Follow the Participant Guide step by step — surveys and evaluations included. The course is mobile-friendly, so you can chip away at modules whenever you have time.

  4. Expect check-ins until you're done

    A facilitator will check in with you regularly from the day you sign up. Aim to finish within your cohort's window (1 cohort = 2-4 weeks).

  5. Stuck? Finish live instead

    You can re-join any weekly webinar and ride along from Part 2 as a guided finish — same course, same completion, extra support.
